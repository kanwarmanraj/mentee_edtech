Who are we ?


Mentee Academy is an online education portal that provides microcontent study videos for students preparing for IAS, IPS, IRS, IFS, CDS, NDA, etc., Govt. exams. Complete with everyday Newspaper analysis, previous year questions, interactive exercises, practice tests, and expert help, we endeavor to make Govt. job exam preparation easy for students and help them to crack.


We also provide resources, subject-wise synopses, and chapter-wise revision videos for all Govt. exams to thoroughly understand concepts from essential to advanced difficulty.


Our products are carefully designed to ensure maximum learning through proven techniques such as conceptual videos and elimination techniques. Mentee Academy aims to make education accessible to all parts of the country with our affordable online videos
